How this works
1. Upload a receipt image or PDF
2. The app extracts or OCRs the text
3. It fills the fields for you
4. You correct anything that looks off
5. Save and export to Excel
OCR is never perfect. Clean, bright, flat receipts work best. Always review extracted values before exporting.
